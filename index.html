Name:Smart House System

Basic Idea: Energy efficiency and security are of highest concern in recent times. We propose 'Home Security' system and smart lighting as an important feature of modern sophisticated residential and office leaving. These must be affordable, reliable and effective. Home security system involves features like fire, smoke, electric door lock, intruders, etc. Smart lighting help in energy conservation and thus prevents consumption of lighting energy during unnecessary hours of the day. We implement PIR sensor to detect motion and GSM module for sending message to the police as well as to the related owner of the building in case of intruder alert.


Social Relevance: Security is one of the main concern of the recent times. In present world of technological advancement proper care should be taken to maintain security and comfort of homes, offices, banks, etc. In this project we have designed a simple but very efficient home security and lighting system. This device has a function of messaging the owner on his/her mobile number in case of an intruder alert and saving energy from unnecessary lighting. The project takes into consideration the existing products and merge them together to create a smart house which not only conserves energy but also take into consideration the security features.
